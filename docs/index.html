Features

Everything you need to track the markets without leaving your terminal.

📊

Candlestick Charts

Braille-dot OHLC candlesticks with wicks, volume bars, and axis labels. Three chart styles to choose from.

⏱

8 Timeframes

Switch between 1D, 1W, 1M, 6M, 1Y, 3Y, 5Y, and 10Y with a single keypress. Charts adapt automatically.

💰

Financials

Revenue vs Earnings charts, key metrics, balance sheet, EPS trend, and valuation ratios (EV, P/S, EV/EBITDA).

📈

Analyst Data

Price targets with upside/downside, consensus ratings, and a stacked recommendation trend chart.

🏛

Holders

Institutional ownership breakdown with visual bars, top institutional holders, and insider holdings.

🔗

Related Symbols

Auto-discovered similar stocks shown with sparkline charts synced to your current timeframe.

🔍

Symbol Search

Search Yahoo Finance with live preview sparklines. Add to your watchlist with Enter.

🌙

Extended Hours

Pre-market and post-market pricing displayed when regular markets are closed.

⚡

Local Cache

15-min TTL for quotes and charts, 24-hour TTL for financials. Offline fallback with stale cache.

🧠

AI Command Window

Press a to open an interactive prompt. Embed slash macros like /symbol:NVDA, /range:1M, /earning:AAPL, /news:TSLA, /portfolio to inject structured context. Missing earnings and news data are auto-fetched on demand (news merges Yahoo Finance + Google News RSS). Output is rendered as markdown with tables. Works with OpenAI, GitHub Copilot (personal / Business / Enterprise), and Google Vertex AI.

📂

Watchlist Groups

Organize symbols into named groups like “Tech”, “Index & ETF”, “Asia”. Switch instantly with number keys 1–9.

💼

Portfolio

Track real positions with live market value, daily and unrealized P/L. Press a to launch the AI Command window pre-seeded with the selected holding and your full portfolio context, or A for a holistic review. Stored privately in ~/.config/finsight/portfolio.yaml with 0600 permissions.

🎨

Color Schemes

8 built-in themes: Default, TokyoNight, Catppuccin, Dracula, Nord, Gruvbox, Solarized, and ANSI 16-color.
